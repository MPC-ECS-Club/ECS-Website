About Us

We are MPC's Engineering and Computer Science Club! We dedicate ourselves to the goal of STEM education at MPC. Along with working on projects during the school year, we also serve as an excellent liaison between STEM students and fruitful internship opportunities.


How to Join

If you are interested in computer science, engineering, or building cool things, this club if for you! In order to join, please fill out Google Google Form, and more details will be sent to you then. (Such as the Discord and GitHub links.) We hope to see you at our next meeting!
